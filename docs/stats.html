Hans Swenson Hitachi HTA Industry Throughout recorded history, the most potent marker of human progress has been our technological and scientific development. As a society we now know more about our world and ourselves than has ever been known on earth. Every one of the brilliant scientists who have contributed to this goal would agree that their research was done to further the knowledge of humanity in general, and this is only possible when their information is freely accessible to anyone who would care to learn. Paywalling science is in direct contrast to our mission as a species, and it should be vehemently opposed by any who is on the side of the people of earth. Please do not allow Elsevier and other power-hungry organizations to maintain their stranglehold on the distribution of science. Thank you
